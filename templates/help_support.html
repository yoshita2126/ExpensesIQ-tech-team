❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new transaction?
Go to the Dashboard, fill in the transaction details (amount, category, type), and click Add. You can also use the Transactions page for bulk entries.
How do I set up a budget?
Navigate to the Budgets section, click "Create Budget", select a category, set your spending limit, and define the time period.
Can I export my data?
Yes! Go to Preferences, enable "Data Export", and you'll receive a CSV file with all your transactions and financial data.
How do I reset my password?
Click on your Profile in Settings, scroll to "Change Password", enter your current and new password, then click Change Password.
What is a spending goal?
Goals allow you to set savings targets. You can create multiple goals with different target amounts and deadlines to track your progress.
